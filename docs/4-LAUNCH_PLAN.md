## PART 5: TESTFLIGHT BETA STRATEGY

**Reality Check:** Our personal network gives us ~10 testers max. That's fine â€” quality feedback from 10 engaged soccer parents/players beats 2,000 passive testers. One focused phase.

### Beta Phase: Inner Circle (2-3 weeks) â€” ~10 testers

- You + family + close friends who are actively in youth soccer
- Mix of parents AND players (kids are power users â€” they'll find bugs fast)
- Focus areas for each tester:
  - Search for their own team â€” did they find it? Was data accurate?
  - Check league standings for their division â€” correct teams, correct points?
  - Try a match prediction â€” intuitive? Results make sense?
  - Use on different devices (iPhone models, iPad)
  - Note anything confusing, broken, or missing
- Build cadence: Every 2-3 days based on feedback
- Success criteria: <1% crash rate, every tester finds their team, no blocking bugs

### Feedback Collection

- Group text / iMessage thread (fastest for 10 people)
- Sentry for automatic crash reporting
- Ask 3 questions after 1 week:
  1. "Did you find your kid's team?"
  2. "What's the one thing you'd change?"
  3. "Would you tell another soccer parent about this?"

### When to Launch (Exit Criteria)

- Zero crashes in latest build over 5+ days
- All testers found their team successfully
- No "this is broken" feedback outstanding
- Onboarding flow works start to finish
