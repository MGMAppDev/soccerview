Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2025

SoccerView ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SoccerView mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

SoccerView is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance, such as which features are most used and app crash reports.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (device type, operating system version) for compatibility purposes.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not require account creation to use the app
  • We do not collect payment information

Data Sources

The soccer team rankings, match results, and statistics displayed in SoccerView are compiled from publicly available sources. This data pertains to youth soccer clubs and their publicly reported match results.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Expo/React Native: For app functionality and updates
  • Sentry: For crash reporting and error tracking (anonymous data only)

Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting any data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

While SoccerView displays information about youth soccer teams, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app displays publicly available team statistics and does not collect data from individual players or users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
