## Method 2: Organization Settings
1. Click your **profile icon** (top right corner)
2. Select **Organization settings** (not project settings)
3. Look for **Billing** tab
4. Click **Upgrade** or **Change plan**

## Method 3: Project Overview
1. Go to **Home** / **Dashboard** main page
2. Look for your project card
3. You might see a banner or button: **"Upgrade to Pro"** or **"Your project is over limits"**
4. Click that banner/button

## Method 4: Via Usage/Limits Warning
1. Go to **Settings** → **Usage**
2. You should see warnings about:
   - Database size: 1149 MB / 500 MB (over limit)
3. Click the **Upgrade** button in the warning

## Method 5: Support Chat
If none of these work:
1. Click the **chat/help icon** (bottom right of dashboard)
2. Type: "I need to upgrade to Pro tier"
3. Support will send you direct upgrade link

## What to Look For

You're looking for one of these:
- ✅ "Upgrade to Pro" button
- ✅ "Change Plan" button
- ✅ Pricing/Billing section
- ✅ Warning about database over limits
- ✅ "Free" tier badge with upgrade option
